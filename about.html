About Us

Have a Creative Exposure

Kongu School of Architecture (KSA) was established in the year 2015 by Kongu Vellalar Institute of Technology Trust at Kongu Engineering College campus. This is the sixth institute of this Trust. Kongu School of Architecture is functioning in the campus of Kongu Engineering College and utilizing its amenities and services for the benefit of architecture students. Kongu School of Architecture is approved by Council of Architecture, New Delhi (TN-71) and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai (College Code: 7334). Sanctioned intake from the academic year 2015 - 2016 is 40. The classes commenced from 10th August 2015.

Why Choose Us

Our Mission

  • To create best architects incorporating good human values.
  • To prepare students to be a pioneer in design and planning of built environment.
  • To develop ethical values and responsibilities among students for nation building .

Our Vision

To provide value based education to nurture and inspire students for design practice and explore knowledge to meet global requirements.

Quality Policy

  • Providing value-based quality education for the development of students as competent and responsible citizens.
  • Contributing to the nation and beyond through research and development.
  • Continuously improving our services.
