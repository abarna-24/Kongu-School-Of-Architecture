    About Us

    Have a Creative Exposure

    Kongu School of Architecture (KSA) was established in the year 2015 by Kongu Vellalar Institute of Technology Trust at Kongu Engineering College campus. This is the sixth institute of this Trust. Kongu School of Architecture is functioning in the campus of Kongu Engineering College and utilizing its amenities and services for the benefit of architecture students.

    Kongu School of Architecture is approved by Council of Architecture, New Delhi (TN-71) and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai (College Code: 7334). Sanctioned intake from the academic year 2015 - 2016 is 40. The classes commenced from 10th August 2015.
