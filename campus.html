Learning Facilities



Studio Cum Lecture Hall


  • Each Studio cum lecture hall has a well-lit airy studio and lecture space grouped together with the floor area of 2000 sq. ft.
  • The space is efficient and encourages students to use it flexibly and make it their own.
  • This imparts flexibility in teaching and learning methods and encourages healthy interaction amongst faculty and students.


Library


  • The college library is a knowledge haven that enriches teaching, learning and research experience for the students and staff.
  • It is well stocked with more than 2000 books, 100+ e-books, several design magazines and E-journal subscriptions on varied subjects like Design, Graphics, Construction, Interiors, Landscape, Architectural Theories and several others.


Computer Lab


  • The College encourages students to envisage, develop and represent their ideas in innovative ways
  • To support this vision, the computer lab has forty computers equipped with the latest design and visualization software.


Art Studio, Survey Lab, Climatology Lab


  • KSA includes a dedicated Art Studio with all the crafting tools and painting tools with generous light required for the artistic space.
  • The surveying lab has equipment to train the students on various methods of land survey.
  • The college has a wood and a metal workshop with necessary machines and tools. This gives an opportunity to students to explore various techniques, materials and also gain valuable hands-on experience.


Model Making Workshop, Material Museum


  • The college has a wood and a metal workshop with necessary machines and tools. This gives an opportunity to students to explore various techniques, materials and also gain valuable hands-on experience.
  • Along with theoretical knowledge, to ensure a well-rounded education a material museum has been set up. The museum has latest material samples, catalogues, application brochures and joinery prototypes.

Exhibition Hall


  • We at KSA, value the creativity, talent and efforts of all our students. We have exhibition and display areas to showcase work of students from time to time. This encourages students and promotes exchange of ideas across various classes in the Institute.


Open CourtYard


  • An open courtyard for group activities, gatherings and cultural events.
  • The entire campus has also been equipped with wireless internet with 450 Mbps leased line access for its staff and students within the premises.
  • The building is planned to give a barrier free access to all its spaces for physically challenged students. There is provision for ramps, elevators, wide lobbies and doors to ensure ease of movement.
  • The staffroom is a comfortable space for the faculty. The Principal’s cabin and staffroom are always open for students.
